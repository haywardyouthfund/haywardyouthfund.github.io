Scholarship Applications

The Hayward Youth Commission, Hayward Youth Fund Sub-Committee, want to help Hayward students who live within the Hayward city limits or within the boundaries of the Hayward Unified School District attain their dream education. We do not ask for student’s GPA or grades, and everyone who applies has the chance to win prizes through a randomized drawing! For more information and up to date application updates, visit haywardyouthfund.com.
The scholarship can be applied to online at tinyurl.com/hyf2018 or on paper/pdf at tinyurl.com/hyf2018pdf or the City Clerk’s Office at the 4th floor of City Hall (777 B Street). The 2018 Hayward Youth Fund Scholarship Application is due on June 30th, 2018 at 11:59 pm. The paper/pdf scholarship can also be postmarked to,
Zachary Ebadi 777 B St. Hayward, CA 94541
emailed to vivianphung@outlook.com, or dropped off (by closing) at the Hayward City Clerk’s Office by the due date. If emailed, please use the subject “Hayward Youth Fund – (your full name)”. Thank you for applying! We look forward to reading your application. Decisions will be released mid-July.
