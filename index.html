Partnership Levels

The Hayward Youth Commission appointed through the Hayward City Council is seeking funds for our annual scholarship for high-achieving Hayward students pursuing a vocational or four- year education. To make this possible, the Hayward Youth Commission wants to partner with you--Hayward businesses and individuals--to create the Hayward Scholarship Fund. All donations will go towards Hayward’s youth and be tax deductible. If you become our partner, we will help market your business in the following ways:

Small Hayward Partner

ANY

  • The Hayward Scholarship Fund Website will indefinitely have sponsors of previous years on the 2018 Hayward Scholarship Fund Sponsor Page.

Hayward Partner

$300+

  • Displayed on 2018 Hayward Scholarship Fund Website which will be shared on all Official Hayward Social Media Accounts AND Hayward Public High School.

  • Displayed on 2018 Hayward Scholarship Fund Banners which will be displayed at all Hayward Public High Schools.

  • Logo Displayed on Every Scholarship Paper Application and Before Entering Every Online Scholarship Application.

  • Small Hayward Partner Rewards with Hayward Partner Designation [larger image and text size]

Loving Partner

$500+

  • If desired, Voting Member on Scholarship Acceptance Committee.

  • Hayward Partner Rewards with Loving Partner Designation [larger image and text size]

Most Valuable Partner (MVP)

$1000+

  • Naming Rights for a 2018 HSF Scholarship: value at partner’s digression but must be less than the donation amount.

  • Loving Partner Rewards with Most Valuable Partner Designation [larger image and text size].
